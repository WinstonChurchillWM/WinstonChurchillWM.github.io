Who is Patrick Mahomes?

Patrick Mahomes is the quaterback for the Kansas City Chiefs. He is widely known for his athletic ability and football IQ.

College Career

Patrick Mahomes went to college at Texas Tech and his head coach was Kliff Kingsbury, the current Arizona Cardinals coach. He played 7 games in his freshman year and in his sophmore and junior year he played 13 and 12 games, respectively (13 due to a bowl game).

The Draft

Patrick Mahomes was selected 10th Overall by the Kansas City Chiefs. He was projected as a 2nd round pick due to his poor footwork and decision making. He was regarded as being drafted high due to the likes of Mitchell Trubisky, Deshaun Watson, and Deshone Kizer in the same draft class. As Sports Illustrated reports, Andy Reid gave the current GM, Mike Veach, a full approval of Patrick. Andy Reid, one of the best offensive minds, gives quaterbacks rigorous tests of skill and football knowledge. Patrick passed the test, and as a result he was the Chief's first pick in the 2017 draft.
